Hamilton was born with fantastic facial hair. And thanks to his unmissable curly white moustache, he's now an internet sensation. Hamilton now has over 100,000 Facebook likes, almost 550,000 Instagram followers, over 10,000 Twitter followers, and has even landed a role in a Friskies music video. But his facial hair isn't the only thing that makes him special - he's also a rescue cat

Hamilton was abandoned in San Fransisco and was just two-years-old when he was put up for adoption and stand-up comedian Jay Stowe fell in love with him at the Human Society Silicon Valley.
